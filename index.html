What is GPX?

GPX is an on-chain price experiment deployed on Solana. The website exists to provide a stable reference point (mint + links + constraints) so anyone can verify what is being discussed.

Rules & Constraints

  • No utility. It is not a product and does not promise functionality.
  • No roadmap. No timeline commitments or “coming soon” statements.
  • No promises. No guarantees of outcomes of any kind.
  • No profit expectations. Not presented as an investment.
  • No marketing language. This page avoids hype by design.

What is being observed?

  • How on-chain price discovery behaves under constraints.
  • How time and market structure influence observed behavior.
  • The behavior of algorithmic and AI-assisted trading activity as an observable on-chain factor.
  • How verifiable references reduce ambiguity in public discussion.

Notes: This site does not publish price, targets, or performance claims.
